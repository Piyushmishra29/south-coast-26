The Four Bases

Four spots. One villa each.

We move down the coast and unpack once per base.

01 · Ahangama

Base camp. Reef breaks, slow mornings, the villa with the long table.

02 · Weligama

The learning bay. Everyone stands up on a wave by sunset. Surf lesson included.

03 · Mirissa

Harbour color and the loudest nights of the trip.

04 · Hiriketiya

The horseshoe cove. Jungle meets swell. We end here on purpose.

The Shape Of The Week

Party. Self. Nature.

Party

Sunset sessions that turn into 3am ones. Mirissa's strip, villa decks, and one night nobody will describe accurately.

Self

One luxury spa session, one sauna and ice bath, and mornings with nothing scheduled. Come back different, or at least well-rested.

Nature

Jungle streams, reef walks, the green interior an hour inland. The coast is the headline; this is the fine print worth reading.

One Of Each Is Included. The Rest Is Up To You.
