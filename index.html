The Shape Of The Week

Party. Self. Nature.

Party

Self

One luxury spa session, one sauna and ice bath, and mornings with nothing scheduled. Come back different, or at least well-rested.

Big shared villas, around twelve bedrooms each, in Ahangama, Weligama and Hiriketiya. Everyone under one roof, not scattered across hotel floors. A pool to fall into, long-table dinners nobody planned, and decks that quietly become the afterparty.
