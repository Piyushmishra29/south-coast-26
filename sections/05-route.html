The Route

Eight days, one coast.

All four towns sit on a 50-km ribbon of the A2 coastal road — every hop a short scooter ride, no travel days lost. September is shoulder season: warm sea, wilder skies, empty lineups.

  1. 01

    TUE · AHANGAMA

    Arrival

    LAND & SETTLE

    Airport pickup and the drive south. Move into the Ahangama villa, claim your rooms, and get toes in the water by sunset. First dinner out together.

  2. 02

    WED · AHANGAMA

    Ease in

    SURF & CAFÉS

    Slow morning, then your included surf lesson at Weligama Bay, a café crawl through Ahangama's roasteries, and the first proper sunset session.

  3. 03

    THU · → WELIGAMA

    Move east

    GOLDEN HOUR

    Checkout, scooter convoy up the coast to the Weligama base. Golden-hour swim, sunset by the bay — the party chapter opens.

  4. 04

    FRI · WELIGAMA

    Beach day

    PARTY NIGHT

    Full beach day, golden-hour swim, then out to the Mirissa beach clubs. It's Friday — this is the first big one.

  5. 05

    SAT · MIRISSA

    The big one

    PARTY & SEA

    A free beach morning — Secret Beach and Coconut Tree Hill — a long lazy afternoon, and then the Saturday everyone flies here for.

  6. 06

    SUN · → HIRIKETIYA

    Reset

    SCENIC DRIVE

    Recovery brunch, pack up, the pretty coastal drive east to Hiriketiya. Into the final villa, sunset yoga, an early night.

  7. 07

    MON · HIRIKETIYA

    Stillness

    NATURE & SELF

    Horseshoe bay, jungle walks, your luxury spa session — a proper wellness day — then a farewell night out and one last look at the coast.

  8. 08

    TUE · DEPARTURE

    Fly home

    LAST SWIM

    Slow morning, a final swim, and the airport transfer home — salt-crusted, sun-drunk and already planning the next one.
